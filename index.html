Clean Energy

Problem

Pollution is a serious threat to our planet. Traditional energy sources like coal, gas, and carbon-based fuels are polluting our environment and harming not just us, but also the future generations. The world is already taking steps to fight pollution, and now it's our responsibility to contribute actively.Clean energy, especially solar energy, is the key to reducing pollution. Unlike coal or gas, solar power produces electricity without releasing harmful gases into the atmosphere. But there's a challenge: Solar energy is still expensive for many middle-class families. To make a real difference, solar energy must be affordable and accessible to everyone.

Idea

Affordable solar energy installation & financing services for homes, shops, schools, and small offices in Pakistan.

Why this will work in Pakistan?

  • .Load shedding and high electricity bills are common
  • Solar technology cost has reduced significantly.
  • Government supports solar adoption (net-metering available).
  • Rising awareness for clean energy and environment.

Services

  • Solar Panel Systems (1kW-10kW)
  • Installation & Maintenance Services
  • Flexible Financing / Installments
  • Mobile App Monitoring (optional advanced feature)

Target Market

  • Residential homes (middle-class families)
  • Small businesses (retail shops, clinics, schools)
  • Areas with high electricity cost/load-shedding

Business Model

  • Product Sales (solar panels, batteries, inverters)
  • Installation Charges
  • Annual Maintenance Packages
  • Monthly Installments/Leasing
  • Referral Commission from Partner Brands
