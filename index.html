Reelate

EAT WHAT YOU WATCH

Problem

In this modren era where everyone want convinience in their life. Social media has become part of our daily life and about 50% user watch food related videos on these platforms.BUT....if they want to eat that particular dish from tat particular placce they have to search it all over the internet or apps like FOODPANDA which is quite time consuming.

Solution

Reelate is a social media platform where foodies can see food related content and also order them while remaining on the same platfrom.The food is delivered to your doorstep through our riders.

USP-Unique Selling Point

  • Combine features of Foodpanda and Tiktok / Youtube Shorts

Services

  • Food related content
  • Food Delivery Service

Target Audience

Users of Social media platforms

How to compete with competitors?

As Reelate is a combination fo two big industries 1. Food and 2. Social media. So its competitors are also big like Foodpanda and Tiktok, Youtube etc , to compete with such big competitors it needs big marketing strategies for example Crumble's marketing strategy.

Business Model

  • Delivery Charges
  • Ads on App and Website
  • Premium Subscription on app
  • A percentage of monthly revenue from the food supplier
